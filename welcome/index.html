Task Feeds

Any Done! User can create a Task Feed. These feeds are configurable workflows that can be augmented with power ups to make them a perfect fit for any situation. All you need to do is create a new feed and add Doers to your Task Feed.

After you add a Doer, you can send those Doers tasks and they will let you know when it is done!

Feed Keys

Done! App is a workflow tool for soft systems. Instances where real people need to get involved and complete physical, social or abstract reasoning tasks â€“ tasks not readily suited to common place digital workflows. Not just anyone that uses Done! App can send you tasks or find you on the network.

Done! is privacy and security first and that is why it is perfect for business and getting stuff done. All you need to to do is generate a unique key and share it with your Task Master. Once your Task Master adds you to a Task Flow it will connect you to that Task Master.

Done! App is secure and lets you optimize your current processes without drastically changing them. Wherever there is a task to be done, Done! App can help.
